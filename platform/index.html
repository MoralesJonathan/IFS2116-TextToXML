How it works

text_fields

Use your OCR-Text

Copy the plain text of your article from your OCR reader of choice and paste it into our text box.

dvr

Follow the instructions

With your text in the text box, follow the on screen instructions to tell us where each part lives within the text.

code

Grab your XML

Once you've followed all the instructions, the program will auto generate the XML with the correct tags ready to be imported to your file.

We did most of the heavy lifting for you to provide the correct syntax and indention for your XML. No need to worry about where or when to put your tags. Just tell the program where your heading, subheading, and paragraph live on the text and we'll auto generate everything you'll need for the class. No headaches, No fees, and No messy XML.
